Crack Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs bonesless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 10oz can RO-TEL
  • 1 cup cooked, chopped bacon
  • 1 1oz package Hidden Valley Ranch Original seasoning
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 Tbsp paprika
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 10oz package cream cheese

Instructions

  1. Place chicken at bottom of slowcooker.
  2. Add all remaining ingredients and stir together, except for cream cheese.
  3. Dice the cream cheese into 8 cubes and place on top of chicken.
  4. Cover with lid and cook on LOW for 6-8 hours.
  5. Use two forks to shred chicken in slow cooker.
  6. Once chicken is shredded, stir to even out consistency.
