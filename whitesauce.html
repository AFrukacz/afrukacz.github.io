"Halal Guys" White Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayonnais (made of only egg yolks, not whole eggs)
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/4 tsp ground caraway
  • 1/4 tsp ground sumac (or lemon pepper if you can't find sumac)
  • 1/4 tsp ground cardamom
  • 2 tsp powdered cumin
  • Just a sprinkle of ground turmeric
  • Pinch of xantham gum
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Whisk mayonnaise, water, and lemon juice together until smooth and no lumps.
  2. Add caraway, sumac, cardamom, and turmeric; stir until combined.
  3. Add xantham gum and mix until it thickens slightly.
  4. Add black pepper to taste.
  5. Store in refrigerator; use liberally.
